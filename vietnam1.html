VIETNAM - VIETNAM WAR
29 April | City

Hi travelers!

On our second stop, we will change of continent. We will fly to the southeast Asian, and as many of this part of the globe, touched by a war, Vietnam War.

Vietnam is a peaceful and welcome country, with very nice and friendly people. But 66 years ago, the country was divided by two due a conflict, the north against the south, with the USA as ally of the south. The war started in 1954 due the French colonial rule and the next year, in 1955 Vietnam was split along the 17th parallel latitude. During the following years, the USA increase their number of troops arguing the Domino Theory, which held that if one Asian country fell to communism, many others would follow. During the 60s, other USA ally countries send troops to South Vietnam to help, as South Korea and Australia. The war develop as an international conflict, it divided the public opinion of the USA by two, while the south was losing battles and troops. Nineteen years later, in 1973, the war ends with the final peace agreement between the United States and the North Vietnam, and the country was reunified as the Socialist Republic of Vietnam.

Nevertheless, the United States lose the war, the north troops develop unique and ingenious techniques to kill the USA troops. One of this examples, the made a hold on the floor with wood nails and covered by leaves. Around it, the hide in small holes where only Vietnamese people fit on it. When an American soldier fells into the hole and is hurt his mates went to rescue him, breaking their protection. At that time, the north Vietnamese soldiers came out of their holes and kill all the unprotected americans soldiers.

This war left 3 millions people death, 58.000 of them americans, and still present if you visit the country, with many military points around the country and many spots to remember it. Even though, Vietnamese people feel proud to be the only one country to win the United States in a war.

Andrea & Cris
