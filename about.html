Hello dear readers, we are two girls with a dream to travel and explore the whole world.

4 years ago, we were two girls focused on university and our job. But one day, after our first travel to Marrakech, we decide to pursue our dream of travelling the whole world living amazing adventures and meeting as much friends as possible. Four years later, we decide to share this dream and all these experiences with you. We believe in global, cooperative and loving people, traveling no matter the odds. Our goal is to help you to achieve your travel dreams sharing our travel guides, adventures, tips and whatever you can need!

Read more

So far, our journey is not very long. However, we have already passed through a few countries that, little by little, have made us reflect on life and on our objectives, to such an extent that we have begun to design a guide for our next trips. The idea has evolved and now the idea is to create a guide to each of the countries of the world. Being us travelers and trusting in the community of travelers, we believe that the best thing is to share it with all of you. What do you think?
